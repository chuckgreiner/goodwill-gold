## Design Language

### Color Palette
- **Primary Gold**: #FFD700 (warm, treasure-like)
- **Accent Orange**: #FF8C42 (energetic, friendly)
- **Teal**: #2A9D8F (modern, trustworthy)
- **Coral**: #E76F51 (playful, warm)
- **Cream**: #FFF8E7 (soft background)
- **Charcoal**: #2B2D42 (text, depth)

### Typography Style
- **Headlines**: Bold, friendly sans-serif (Poppins, Inter, or similar)
- **Body**: Clean, readable (Inter, Open Sans)
- **Accent text**: Slightly playful but professional

## User Personas

### Primary: "Sarah the Side Hustler"
- Age: 28-35
- Goal: Turn thrift shopping into extra income
- Pain point: Doesn't know which brands are valuable
- Motivation: Financial independence, sustainability

### Secondary: "Maya the Maximizer"
- Age: 22-27
- Goal: Build a resale business on Depop/Poshmark
- Pain point: Wastes time on items that don't sell
- Motivation: Entrepreneurship, creative expression

### Tertiary: "Lisa the Leisure Shopper"
- Age: 35-50
- Goal: Find deals and unique pieces for herself
- Pain point: Overwhelmed by thrift store volume
- Motivation: Sustainability, treasure hunting fun

## Competitive Differentiation
- **vs. Manual research**: Instant AI identification vs. hours of Googling
- **vs. Generic resale apps**: Specialized for thrift sourcing, not just selling
- **vs. Price guides**: Real-time marketplace data, not outdated books
- **Community angle**: Social treasure hunting, not isolated shopping
